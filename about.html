About 'Growing Trees at Home'

What is 'Growing Trees at Home'?

Hi, my name is Jenn Taft and I love growing trees! 'Growing Trees at Home' has grown out of a love for seeing tree seeds germinate and grow in to lovely little saplings. I live in the West Midlands, UK and have a garden space that so far has accomodated the growth of over 300 baby trees, using no specialised equipment, just what I had to hand.
